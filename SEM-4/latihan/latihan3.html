Glosarium

Instant Coffee
A type of coffee that is made from dried coffee extract. It is quick and easy to prepare by simply adding hot water.
Matcha
A finely ground powder of specially grown and processed green tea leaves. It is traditionally used in Japanese tea ceremonies and has a vibrant green color and a unique flavor.
Chawan
A traditional Japanese tea bowl used for preparing and drinking matcha.
Chasen
A bamboo whisk used in the preparation of matcha to create a frothy texture.
